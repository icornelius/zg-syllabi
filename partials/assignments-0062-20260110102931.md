For conventions of academic writing consult @ChicagoManualStyle2024 and @BoothCraftResearch2024.
(*The Craft of Research* is available as an e-book from Loyola Libraries.)
Include a complete, accurate, and correctly formatted list of works cited.
Prior to submission run the checks for clarity described in *The Craft of Research*, sections 15.2, 15.3, and 15.5.

Print a paper copy (double-spaced, with page numbers) and upload a PDF to Sakai.
