Submit a working draft of your essay, as a PDF or DOCX file. A paper copy is not required.

The draft should have a title, abstract, section headings, source citations, and bibliography. For guidance on abstracts, see the "Quick Tip" at the end of *Craft of Research*, chapter 11. Sources should be cited in one of the two major Chicago styles: author-date or notes-and-bibliography.

After the abstract, but before the introduction to the essay itself, create a section title "To the reader."
In this section, identify where you are in the writing process.
Is this a full draft or a partial one?
If partial, what part has been drafted and what remains to be done?
What areas do you especially request help with -- sources and quotations, claim, reasons and evidence, argument structure, paragraph structure, sentence grammar and clarity, or something else?
