There are several recent introductions to textual criticisim aimed at the student of English literature:
@AbbottIntroductionBibliographicalTextual2014;
@FraistatCambridgeCompanionTextual2013;
@KelemenTextualEditingCriticism2009;
and (less recent) @GreethamTextualScholarshipIntroduction1994.
All can be used with profit; none are really adequate.
@FraistatCambridgeCompanionTextual2013 and @KelemenTextualEditingCriticism2009 have been placed on [course reserve](https://luc.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/discovery/search?query=any,contains,cornelius%20413&tab=CourseReserves&search_scope=CourseReserves&vid=01LUC_INST:01LUC).

For an indication of current disciplinary priorities and models of scholarly practice, see the [recipients of prizes and awards](https://textualsociety.org/sts-prizes-and-awards/) conferred by the Society for Textual Scholarship.
