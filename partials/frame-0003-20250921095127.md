# General instructions {#general-note-collection}

Create a collection of ten research notes, sourced from a least three different readings or video presentations assigned in the first four weeks of our class.

The note collection and accompanying overview will be submitted in two versions: a paper copy (due in class on Friday, 19 September) and a textually identical digital copy (uploaded to Sakai Assignments).
The digital copy may be PDF, DOCX, TXT, or Markdown format.

Any use of generative AI must be disclosed and justified in the reflective overview: see the detailed instructions, below.
