- develop an ability to perform close readings of literature and other kinds of texts, paying careful attention to textual detail.
- develop understanding of human diversity as it is represented in literature and other kinds of texts.
- develop skills for clear communication of ideas, in writing that employs appropriate word choice, diction, structure, and mechanics.
- develop skills for creating focused and well-supported arguments and analyses.
- develop understanding of literary and cultural theories by incorporating them into their work in precise and effective ways.
- develop an ability to employ in their own creative works their understanding of the conventions and techniques of particular literary genres.
- develop knowledge of textual genres, histories, movements, traditions, and innovations.
- develop an understanding of how texts reflect and affect societies and cultures.
