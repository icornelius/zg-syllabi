The following book is required for this course and available for rent or purchase at the Loyola University Chicago [Lakeshore Campus Bookstore](https://www.bkstr.com/loyolachicagostore/home):

> Black, Joseph, et al., editors. *The Broadview Anthology of British Literature, Volume 1: The Medieval Period.* 3rd ed., Broadview Press, 2015.

If you purchase books from a source other than the campus bookstore, plan ahead to allow for delivery.

Students are reminded that most course readings are protected by copyright and should not be shared outside this course without written permission of the copyright holder.
