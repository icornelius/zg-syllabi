# Claim paragraph

Write one paragraph, introducing your topic, ending with a statement of your claim. Use *Craft of Research*, 10.2.1 ("Sketch a Working Introduction") as a guide.

# Storyboard

Create a story board of your body paragraphs. Use *Craft of Research*, 5.5 ("Planning Your Research Argument") and 10.2.3-4 ("Plan the Body of Your Paper," "Plan Each Section and Subsection") as a guide. For each segment of your story board, identity the operative **reason** and **evidence** and the **key terms** that pertain especially to that section of your argument.
