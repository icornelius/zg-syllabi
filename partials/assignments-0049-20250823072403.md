Each student will produce one collection of structured research notes, on the topic of their [class presentation](#class-presentation).
Notes should be drawn from sources used in the class presentation or from subsequent research.
Note collections are due one week after the presentation date.

Observe the [general instructions for written assignments](#policies-on-written-work).

If you form a group to deliver a collaborative presentation, the note collection should also be done as a group.
Describe the division of labor in the [reflective overview](#collection-overview).
Each group member should submit an identical electronic copy, recording the names of all group members.
The group should submit a single paper copy.
