Required readings and other media are listed on the [Course Schedule](#schedule) and available in one of two forms:

- PDF documents posted to the resources folder on [Sakai][sakai]
- Books, journal articles, and other works published to the open web or available in digital form from Loyola Libraries

In a few cases, on-line resources are linked directly from the course schedule.
More often, the course schedule links to an item in the [Bibliography](#bibliography) at the end of this syllabus.
If the bibliography entry for a required reading gives no URL, you will find a PDF scan in the resources folder on Sakai.

Note:

- For access to digital publications of Oxford University Press, select the option "Sign in through your institution."
- The current edition of *The Chicago Manual of Style* is apparently accessible to us only from an on-campus internet connection.

Students are reminded that many course readings are protected by copyright and should not be shared outside this course without written permission of the copyright holder.
