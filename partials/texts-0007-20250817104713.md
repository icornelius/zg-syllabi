Required readings and other media are listed on the [Course Schedule](#schedule) and available to students in one of three forms:

1. On-line open-access resources linked from the Course Schedule or the [Bibliography](#bibliography)
1. PDF documents posted to the resources folder on [Sakai][sakai] \(marked "Sakai" in the Course Schedule)
1. E-books on electronic [course reserve][course-reserve] \(marked "Reserve" on the Course Schedule)

Students are reminded that many course readings are protected by copyright and should not be shared outside this course without written permission of the copyright holder.

Students are not required to purchase books for this class.
