AI chatbots routinely make sh\*t up.
That is what they are designed to do: they are tools for text generation, not information retrieval.
For information retrieval use a reputable reference resource.
A good first stop is [Oxford Reference](https://www.oxfordreference.com/).

For (mostly) sane advice on current generative AI systems, see @Mollick15TimesUse2025 and @OftenHelpfulSometimes2025.
