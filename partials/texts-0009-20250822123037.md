We use several on-line reference works:

- [The Chicago Manual of Style](https://www.chicagomanualofstyle.org/home.html)
- [Oxford Reference](https://www.oxfordreference.com/)
- [The Middle English Dictionary](https://quod.lib.umich.edu/m/middle-english-dictionary/dictionary)
- [The Oxford English Dictionary](https://www.oed.com)
- [Oxford Bibliographies in Medieval Studies](https://www.oxfordbibliographies.com/browse?module_0=obo-9780195396584)
- [Oxford Bibliographies in British and Irish Literature](https://www.oxfordbibliographies.com/browse?module_0=obo-9780195396584)

These are available at no additional cost to you.
Some have been purchased by Loyola University Libraries for your use (for these you will be prompted to authenticate with your UVID).
Others are served out on the open web by their creators.

For access to the *Oxford English Dictionary*, Oxford Bibliographies, or publications in the Oxford Reference collections, select the option "Sign in through your institution".
