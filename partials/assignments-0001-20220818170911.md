Written assignments should be formatted as follows:

- double-spaced text
- a standard 12-point font
- 1-inch margins
- numbered pages

Written assignments should be submitted in two forms, paper and electronic.
Paper copies are due at the beginning of class.
An identical electronic copy should be uploaded to Sakai (the Assignments tab) in PDF format.
