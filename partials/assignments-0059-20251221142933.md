During our final exam period each group will speak for 5-7 minutes about their project. Briefly discuss both your argument and your process of research and writing.

Your presentation may be supported with slides or other resources. Post a link, or links in the Sakai Wiki.

If you are working in a group, each member should do some of the speaking. Decide the division of labor in advance.

I will keep time by announcing the 5-minute mark and 7-minute mark. Each presentation will be followed by a short question-and-answer period. 

For advice on presentations, see *Craft of Research*, chapter 16. Because these presentations are very short, you will be able to deliver only a portion of your argument. Pay special attention to *Craft of Research*, section 16.3.1 ("Narrow Your Focus").
