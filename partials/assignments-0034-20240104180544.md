Students are expected to participate constructively and thoughtfully in class activities.
Complete the assignments listed on the [Schedule](#schedule) and be prepared to talk about them.
Bring questions and observations.
Informal assignments, which may be announced from time to time, count towards this grade component.
Persistent unexcused absences will reduce your participation grade.
See [Attendance](#attendance).
