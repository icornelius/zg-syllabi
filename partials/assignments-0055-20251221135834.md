This assignment is ungraded but required as a condition for earning points on the final product. There are two parts.

You may type or paste text into the window below, attach a file, or direct me to a GitHub repository.

# Part 1: Topic, Question, and Problem

Write a one-paragraph research proposal, stating your topic, question, and problem, as described in chapters 1 and 2 of *The Craft of Research*.

Your paragraph should be composed of complete, grammatically correct sentences. After drafting your paragraph, run the checks for clarity, described in *The Craft of Research*, sections 15.2, 15.3, and 15.5. Revise accordingly. (You previously performed these checks in the revision assignment, steps 41-43 in the step-by-step instructions.)

# Part 2: Process

In one paragraph, describe your process to date and the computing tools you have used for this assignment. Where are you conducting your writing? Do paper and pencil have any role in your writing process? What computer programs are you using? If you are in a group, how are you communicating?
